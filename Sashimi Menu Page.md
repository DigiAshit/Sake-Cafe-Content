## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between Sushi and Sashimi?
Sashimi is simply fresh, premium raw fish sliced and served on its own without rice. Sushi (or Nigiri) is sliced fish served over a small bed of seasoned sushi rice.

### Is all sashimi raw?
Most sashimi is raw, like our Tuna and Yellowtail. However, options like Eel (Unagi) and Crab Stick are cooked!

### Can I get a sashimi combination platter?
While these are a la carte orders of 4 pieces, our chefs frequently prepare large, beautiful mixed platters for guests upon request!

### What pairs best with sashimi?
Sashimi is best enjoyed with a light touch of soy sauce, a dab of wasabi, and a refreshing sake or light Japanese beer.
