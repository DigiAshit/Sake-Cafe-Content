## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do you offer vegetarian sushi rolls?
Yes! We offer a fresh Vegetable Roll, Cucumber Roll, and Avocado Maki.

Absolutely. Just let your server know, and we can substitute soy paper for nori on any of our classic rolls.

### Are these rolls available for takeout?
